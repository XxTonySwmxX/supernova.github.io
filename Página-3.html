UTILITY:

We’ve been in the CNFT scene for quite some time and while we’ve seen many great projects, we’ve also spotted an opportunity in the current market to give our community a chance for tangible returns. We’ve decided to not go down the route of promising a game, building a metaverse or creating a native token but are committing to giving you value, awesome rewards and returns for your NFT(s). 

A substantial part of our budget has been allocated to be dropped back to our holders. Royalties play a big part as well, 100% of our royalties will be given back to lucky holders through biweekly draws. This will be in the form of scheduled monthly Bluechip NFT drops, monthly ADA giveaways, and biweekly royalty giveaways. In order to be eligible for drops, your NFT cannot be listed during the draw. NFT’s purchased without smart contracts and thus not paying royalties will not be eligible. The draws will happen at random times on Fridays (GMT). 

We built out a clear drops calendar and we’re ready to execute on it! We do not want to keep our holders guessing or waiting for what we are planning to do next, therefore we ensure you KNOW what’s coming next and WEN it’s coming! 

Every first Friday of the month we will drop 1 Bluechip NFT to 1 lucky holder. The winner will be randomly selected, and holders of an NFT with higher rarity will have more chances to win.

Every third Friday of the month, 2K ADA will be dropped to the winner of that week. All holders get 1 entry every 2K ADA drop.

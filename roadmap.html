We’re happy to present you our roadmap full of exciting drops and surprises.

UTILITY:

We’ve been in the CNFT scene for quite some time and while we’ve seen many great projects, we’ve also spotted an opportunity in the current market to give our community a chance for tangible returns. We’ve decided to not go down the route of promising a game, building a metaverse or creating a native token but are committing to giving you value, awesome rewards and returns for your NFT(s).

A substantial part of our budget has been allocated to be dropped back to our holders. We scheduled monthly Bluechip NFT drops and monthly ADA giveaways. Royalties play a big part as well, 100% of our royalties will be given back to lucky holders through biweekly draws. In order to be eligible for drops, your NFT cannot be listed during the draw. NFT’s purchased without smart contracts and thus not paying royalties will not be eligible. The draws will happen at random times on Fridays (GMT).

Tell me more about the drops?

Every first Friday of the month we will drop 1 Bluechip NFT to 1 lucky holder. The winner will be randomly selected, and holders of an NFT with higher rarity will have more chances to win. On top of this we'll also drop Bluechip NFT's on selected holidays. We'll update the events tab in Discord so you know which holidays to look forward to.

The two Supernova lead (1/1's) characters will get 10 entries every Bluechip drop
Legendary holders get 5 entries every Bluechip drop
Epic holders get 4 entries every Bluechip drop
Rare holders get 3 entries every Bluechip drop
Uncommon holders get 2 entries every Bluechip drop
Common holders get 1 entry every Bluechip drop
Every third Friday of the month, 2K ADA will be dropped to the winner of that week. All holders get 1 entry every 2K ADA drop.

Every third Friday of the month, 2K ADA will be dropped to the winner of that week. All holders get 1 entry every 2K ADA drop. 

We want to keep the community airdrops exciting, so every community drop held on the 2nd and 4th Friday of every month, 5 holders will receive 100% of the royalties. If we have a secondary volume of 100k ADA in 2 weeks this will be divided as follows:

100k ADA is 20k ADA in royalties.
8000 ADA for 1 legendary
6000 ADA for 1 epic holder
3000 ADA for 1 rare holder
2000 ADA for 1 uncommon holder
1000 ADA for 1 common holder

Expect at least 4 drops per month!

Supernova is a Cardano Based Lore & NFT project 

We’re a team of close friends and passionate artists & devs. that have a combined 20+ years of experience in tech. We’ve built a project with high quality art, immersive storytelling and sick drops.

We’ve committed to creating original art of high quality. Together with our artists we’ve worked tirelessly for months to create this art from scratch. All our characters were firstly hand drawn and later generated in 3D. The weapons, armor and features of each Mech were carefully selected from our Supernova series and created as a trait for you to enjoy!

The Supernova story will be presented in a unique way, together with our lore writer we’ve created a story that takes you on a rollercoaster of events in a world on the brink of extinction. This will be presented to you in a 12 episode animated series with the first episode to drop shortly after launch. 

Our roadmap is filled with excitement, so our holders always have something to look forward to. We’ve made the strategic decision to drop the best NFT’s, 100% royalties and huge monthly ADA drops to winning holders.We built in a timer on our website so you’ll always know when the next drop will take place.

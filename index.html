Supernova is a Cardano Based Lore & NFT project.
We’re a team of close friends and passionate artists & devs. that have a combined 20+ years of experience in tech. We’ve built a project with high quality art and immersive storytelling for you to enjoy! Our long-term goal is to build a strong community, keep delivering amazing art and storytelling that will keep you invested in our world. We hope you stick around to see what more we have in store for you! 

We’ve committed to creating original art of high quality. Together with our artists we’ve worked tirelessly for months to create this art from scratch. All our characters were firstly hand drawn and later generated in 3D. The Supernova story will be presented in a unique way, together with our lore writer we’ve created a story that takes you on a rollercoaster of events in a world on the brink of extinction.
